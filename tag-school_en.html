Why? What for? And how?

TAG. school is a creative approach to bartending. It is almost 100 m2 of space that allows for comfortable training and includes a part for cooking and mixing cocktails, as well as a part for relaxing and spending time together.

We want to share our knowledge and experience with bartenders, offering two different training levels, and also with cocktail enthusiasts looking for an alternative for spending time with their friends!

Bartending training is characterized by following the trends, new techniques of preparing cocktails and a creative approach to creating concepts.

In addition, we also have an offer for people who love cocktails and at the same time are looking for an alternative to spending time with friends or organizing a birthday surprise for a loved one in a unique and private space.

TAG. school is a place where you can prepare cocktails together and a relaxation zone where you will have a good time together!

Courses

1. Beginning

Bartending training for people who start their adventure with bartending or have no development opportunities, but their manager wants to invest in them. It is a two-day course where we will learn:

  • basic knowledge of alcohols, bartending equipment and cocktail preparation techniques
  • cocktail ingredients such as syrups, infusions and cordials
  • how to balance cocktails with their garnishes and the way of serving
  • hospitality - how to take care of your guests

Training dates will appear on our Facebook page. If you would like an individually adapted training date and plan to your needs, write to us by e-mail!

2. Level Up!

Training for people with experience working behind a bar who want to develop further, as well as for bar chefs, managers and head bartenders who want to become a role model for their team. We also offer comprehensive preparation for bartending competitions. What will we do during the training?

  • salt and smoke in cocktails
  • fermentation and texture of cocktails - milkwash, fatwash, whey, wax
  • carbonization - bubbles in a cocktails
  • temperature and how it affects on taste
  • contemporary trends in bartending
  • Competition at the very end of training for every participant.

Training dates will appear on our Facebook page. If, on the other hand, you would like an individually adapted training date and plan to your needs, write to us by e-mail!

3. Cocktails at home

Trainings taking place in our spacious kitchen, where we can teach simple methods of preparing cocktails for up to 16 people at a common table. It is a great alternative to corporate integrations. Learn how to make cocktails to surprise your friends at your house party!

4. House party

Almost 100 m2 of space, a common table where you can prepare cocktails together and area where you can enjoy them together! An alternative to going to the cinema with friends or surprise parties for friends - an unique and private space for you and your loved ones.
