WHO WE ARE

TAG. is a cocktail bar based on classic cocktails with a light modern twist. The interior looks like an art gallery, where you can comfortably sit in the chair with a cocktail in your hand.

TAG. is a symbol left by graffiti artists, which is their hallmark. A symbol of creativity and unconventionality, which we also apply to our twists on classic cocktails.

The frame is just the beginning for us to create something unique.

MENU

Our cocktail menu is based on five classic cocktails and five modern twists. Our goal is to show very simple flavors in an interesting way, creating unconventional creations. We encourage you to try both the classic and the TAG. version to see the differences between them. In cocktails you will find, among others, the aroma of fresh cut grass, lime leaves, salty banana and coffee combined with strawberry. Remember that we have nearly 100 bottles at the bar, which we will gladly use to create specially prepared cocktails for you. Just tell us what you like and we'll take of it!
